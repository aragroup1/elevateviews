Generic software makes you work its way. We build around yours.

Off-the-shelf SaaS is built for the average business, so every real business ends up bending its process to fit the tool, paying for features it never uses, and gluing three subscriptions together with spreadsheets. A custom operational system does the opposite: it maps to how your team already works, and removes the steps that waste time.

What a custom operational system covers

  • Order capture across every channel you sell through, in one place
  • Live stock control that updates as things move, not at month end
  • Reordering driven by real usage, with supplier details built in
  • Roles and permissions so the right people see the right screens
  • Reports that answer the questions you actually ask

Built on a proven core, then tailored

We do not start from a blank page for every client. We build on a tested core and tailor it to your workflow, which keeps the cost down and the first working version close. You get something live and earning in weeks, then we extend it.

Who this is for

Owners and operators who have outgrown spreadsheets and paper, who lose hours to admin, or who are paying for several tools that still do not talk to each other.

FAQ

Common questions.

How is this different from buying software off the shelf?

Off-the-shelf software forces your process to match the tool. A custom system matches the tool to your process, so there is nothing to work around and nothing you pay for but never use.

Is custom software expensive?

Less than most owners expect. We build on a proven core and tailor it, rather than starting from scratch, so a focused system costs far less than a full bespoke build.

Can it connect to tools I already use?

Usually yes. Part of the mapping stage is deciding what stays, what connects by integration, and what the new system replaces.
