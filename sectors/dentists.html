Recalls are revenue, and they slip

Every patient due a check-up is booked revenue, but recalls slip when they depend on someone remembering to send them. Gaps in the book go unfilled. Surgeries run short on supplies at the worst moment. All three are systems problems.

What we build for dental practices

  • Automated recall reminders that bring patients back on time
  • Scheduling that fills cancellations from a waiting list
  • Supply and consumable stock tracked across surgeries
  • Reordering tied to real usage and lead times
  • Reporting on book utilisation and recall performance

Quiet, reliable, in the background

The best practice systems are invisible. Patients get reminded, gaps get filled, supplies arrive before they run out, and the front desk is freed from chasing.
