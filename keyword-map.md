## Tiered strategy

- **Tier 1 (target first, rankable ~3-6 months):** long-tail, low difficulty, high buyer intent.
- **Tier 2 (months 6-12, needs content):** mid difficulty, commercial.
- **Tier 3 (long game / paid only):** head terms, High difficulty, funded-SaaS dominated. Pillar pages only.
