Worked example

A restaurant that stopped guessing its orders.

Orders arrive across the counter, the phone and three delivery apps. Stock is counted by eye. The owner reorders on a gut feel and writes off what spoils.

We connect the order channels, track stock as it moves, and forecast next week from real sales history and the calendar. The owner reorders to a number, not a guess. Less waste, fewer stockouts, hours back every week.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is this just a website service?

Websites are the entry point. The value is the system underneath: how orders, stock and staff time are managed. We build both.

How much does a custom business system cost?

It depends on scope. A focused ordering or stock system starts lower than most owners expect, because we build on a proven core then tailor it. We scope and quote first.

How long does it take?

A first working version is usually weeks, not months. We get something live and earning, then build out.

Will it work with my current tools?

Usually, yes. The mapping stage finds what stays, what connects and what gets replaced.
